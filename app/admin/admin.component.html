Admin Roles and Responsibilities

As an admin, you are responsible for managing the tours, hotels, bookings, and users of this platform. Your roles include:

  • Managing and updating tour information
  • Overseeing hotel listings and details
  • Handling user bookings and ensuring they are up-to-date
  • Managing user accounts and their permissions

Rules and Regulations

To maintain a smooth operation, admins must adhere to the following rules:

  • Ensure all data entered is accurate and up-to-date
  • Respect user privacy and maintain confidentiality
  • Report any suspicious activity to the system administrator
  • Follow the guidelines for data management and security protocols
